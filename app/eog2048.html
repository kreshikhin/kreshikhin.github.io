EOG 2048

The game Emergencies of Galaxy 2048 gives you the chance to control an spaceship flying in outer space. You should to overpass meteorite storm and save people caught in an emergency situation. Use a blaster by tapping on screen, turn a device to left and to right for overflight meteorites, press on a screen over or under spaceship to accelerate or brake it.
